Modern CV Guide

What a modern UK CV looks like

Your rewritten CV follows all of these conventions. Here's why each one matters.

Recommended layout order
1
Personal profile (3–4 lines)
2
Core skills / Key competencies
3
Work experience (most recent first)
4
Education & qualifications
5
Professional development / training
Do's and don'ts
🚫
No photo or date of birth — not used in modern UK CVs and can trigger unconscious bias.
🚫
No "References available on request" — assumed and wastes precious space.
🚫
No objective statement — replace with a punchy 3–4 line personal profile.
✅
Skills section near the top — ATS systems scan for keywords early in the document.
✅
Quantify your achievements — "Managed a team of 8, reducing costs by 15%" beats "Managed a team".
✅
Single-column layout — two-column designs often break ATS parsers.
✅
Keep to 2 pages — anything more won't be read.
✅
Mirror the job ad's exact keywords — ATS matches language precisely.
✅
Tailor every application — one CV sent to 50 jobs gets 0 responses.
